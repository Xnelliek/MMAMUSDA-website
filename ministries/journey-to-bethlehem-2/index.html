Community Service

Community Service

This inter-active journey through a live re-enactment of the birth of Jesus is an exciting way to explore the true Christmas story. Starting in the village of Jerusalem with an outdoor market, exploring the wise mens’ study of ancient prophecies, attending an audience with King Herod, following the star with the shepherds through the local inn and to a lowly manger will enhance your understanding of the Savior’s birth. Then as you continue the journey you will learn more about Christ’s crucifixion, resurrection, and second coming.

This live re-enactment takes place during the first weekend of December at the Maasai Mara University church.

Come join us.  Community Service promises to be a memorable time for you and your family!
