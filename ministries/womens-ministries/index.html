Women’s Ministries

Encouragement is a precious commodity that sometimes is in short supply. Our local women’s ministry team brings hope and joy through events that strengthen the fabric of our togetherness. Understanding and discovery keep our hearts fresh and courageous for the special work God has given the women in our midst.
