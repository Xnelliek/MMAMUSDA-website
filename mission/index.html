Missions

Missions

The Maasai Mara University church believes its strength should bless other churches around the world. Over the past 16 years the church has facilitated 850 short term foreign mission experiences for the members of the Maasai Mara University Church, our friends. We have funded approximately 50 churches in Kenya and personally built another 20 churches, schools and homes for people and congregations in need throughout Central and SouthWestern Kenya.

The blessings of service have bonded the hearts of the Maasai Mara University members together in ways that ordinary socializing could never do. The dental and medical work that we offer has been a beacon of hope to otherwise medically deprived constituencies. Furthermore, the evangelistic meetings and Vacation Bible Schools have left God’s Word in the hearts of many both young and old alike.
